A paragraph is a distinct section of writing, typically consisting of several sentences, that focuses on a single idea or topic. It is used to organize and structure written work, making it easier for readers to follow the author's thoughts. Key components usually include a topic sentence that states the main idea, supporting sentences that elaborate on it, and often a concluding sentence that summarizes or transitions.
